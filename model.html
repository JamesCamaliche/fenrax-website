The Fenrax Model

The Assets
Already Exist.

Every community is surrounded by helicopters, heavy equipment, boats, and skilled personnel that go completely unmapped in a disaster — sitting idle while first responders are overwhelmed. Fenrax changes that.

The Problem

An Invisible Inventory.

When disaster strikes, communities rely almost entirely on government-owned assets. But those assets are expensive to acquire, maintain, and staff — and there are never enough of them.

Meanwhile, the resources needed for an effective response already exist in the community. Private aircraft. Construction equipment. Rescue boats. Doctors and engineers and licensed operators. They're just not in the plan.

No one has mapped them. No one has verified them. No one has connected them to the people who need them. Until a crisis hits — and by then it's too late.

The Fenrax Model

Integrate. Activate.
Respond.

Fenrax's goal is simple: take the assets that already exist in a community and integrate them into the local disaster response plan — so they're live, verified, and available for community response 24/7.

We identify privately-owned assets across every category. We verify them. We build them into real municipal emergency plans. And when a disaster hits, the right assets reach the right place — with the right people operating them.

This isn't about building new government capacity. It's about unlocking what every community already has, and making it count when it matters most.

01
Identify

AI-powered discovery of underutilized local assets — helicopters, trucks, equipment, boats, personnel — building a verified, living inventory across the community.

02
Verify

Every asset is confirmed. Every operator is credentialed. Capabilities, certifications, and availability are matched to specific disaster response scenarios.

03
Integrate

Assets are embedded directly into municipal Emergency Operations Plans — live, current, and ready to be called on the moment a disaster is declared.

04
Mobilize

When disaster strikes, FENRAX dispatches the right assets to the right place. Verified operators are matched to equipment — ownership doesn't limit response.

Always On

Live. Verified.
Available 24/7.

The Fenrax platform isn't activated at the moment of disaster — it's live before it ever happens. Response plans are always current. Assets are always tracked. Operators are always verified.

Pre-Event
Built Before the Crisis

Asset databases, operator registries, and response plans are built, tested, and exercised before any disaster occurs. Annual training keeps participants ready.

During Event
Instant Activation

When a disaster is declared, FENRAX activates the pre-built plan. Assets are dispatched, operators are notified, and insurance coverage engages automatically.

Continuous
Always Updating

The platform continuously monitors asset availability, operator status, and community conditions — so the response plan always reflects current ground truth.
